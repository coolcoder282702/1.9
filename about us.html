About Us

St.John Bosco Study Hub was created with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, accessible educational resources for Year 5 students in SJKT Dual Language Programme (DLP) schools across Malaysia. Our goal is to supplement classroom learning with free study notes, quizzes, and a supportive community to help students excel in their studies.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to succeed. Our platform is a collaborative effort by a dedicated team of educators and volunteers who are passionate about empowering the next generation of learners. By focusing on key subjects like Mathematics, Science, and languages, we aim to build confidence and foster a love for learning.

Our Logo

Our logo is a visual representation of our core values and mission. It combines the image of St. John Bosco, a patron of students, with various educational elements. The vibrant colors and dynamic design reflect our commitment to making learning an engaging and inspiring experience for all students. Each element in the logo, from the books to the students, symbolizes our dedication to holistic education and community support.
